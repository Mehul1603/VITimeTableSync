# VITimeTableSync: VIT Timetable Integration for Google Calendar

This app adds your classes schedule to your google calendar.

Just copy paste the text in vtop time table section.

**Upcoming features:**
1. Adds exam schedule to your calendar.
2. Automatically detects holidays and changes your calendar accordingly.
